About the job
About the Company:

My client is a innovative startup focused on revolutionizing the industrial sector through the power of artificial intelligence. By combining cutting-edge computer vision technology with advanced machine learning algorithms, we provide solutions that enhance productivity, improve safety, and drive operational efficiency across various industries. As we continue to grow, we are looking for a talented Computer Vision Engineer to join our dynamic team and contribute to our mission of transforming the industrial landscape.
Role Overview:

The Computer Vision Engineer will play a crucial role in developing and deploying AI-powered vision systems that address real-world industrial challenges. This individual will work closely with a team of AI researchers, software developers, and industry experts to design, implement, and optimize computer vision models. The ideal candidate is passionate about solving complex problems, has a strong technical background in computer vision, and is eager to apply their skills in a fast-paced startup environment.
Key Responsibilities:

Develop and implement computer vision algorithms for object detection, tracking, segmentation, and classification in industrial settings.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate computer vision solutions into existing products and systems.
Optimize algorithms for performance, scalability, and robustness, ensuring reliable operation in diverse industrial environments.
Conduct research and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in computer vision and AI, applying new techniques and methodologies to improve product offerings.
Participate in the full software development lifecycle, from concept and design to testing and deployment.
Analyze and interpret data to continuously improve the accuracy and efficiency of vision systems.
Qualifications:

Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or a related field, with a focus on computer vision or machine learning.
Proven experience in developing computer vision applications, preferably in industrial or real-world environments.
Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, C++, or MATLAB, and experience with computer vision libraries like OpenCV, TensorFlow, or PyTorch.
Strong understanding of image processing, feature extraction, and deep learning techniques.
Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team.
Familiarity with hardware integration and edge computing is a plus.
